04 / CASE STUDIES

Delivering Metrics That Matter

CAC OPTIMIZATION

THE PROBLEM

Customer acquisition costs were climbing faster than Lifetime Value could support. Sales wanted more leads, business wanted lower spend. The real constraint wasn't volume, it was efficiency.

THE STRATEGIC SOLUTION:

Instead of cutting spend, I shifted allocation. Replaced expensive paid channels with high-leverage organic plays—lifecycle email that activated dormant users, referral mechanics that turned customers into acquisition channels, and content that ranked organically. Preserved growth velocity while improving unit economics.

Lower CAC Maintained Growth

Cost Efficiency

THE BUSINESS PROBLEM

Event sponsorships consumed significant budget but delivered unclear ROI. Brand visibility was high. Pipeline impact was invisible. CFO wanted accountability.

THE STRATEGIC SOLUTION:

Rebuilt event strategy from a P&L lens. Calculated cost-per-acquisition at each event. Mapped every activation to a revenue outcome—merchant sign-ups, partnership conversations, contract renewals. Cut events that didn't deliver commercial value. Doubled down on those that did. Transformed brand spend into measurable demand generation.

Eliminated Wasteful Spend Direct Revenue Attribution
